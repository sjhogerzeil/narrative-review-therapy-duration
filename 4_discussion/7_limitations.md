## Key limitations to address

- Not a systematic review — search is structured but not exhaustive or replicable in the PRISMA sense
- Heterogeneous evidence types — no formal method for weighting academic vs. experiential sources
- Single reviewer — no inter-rater reliability for annotation or data extraction
- Survivorship bias across all layers (see 4_discussion/2_survivorship_bias.md)
- Working definition of healing is deliberately broad — limits precision of duration estimates
- English-language and Western bias — non-Western healing traditions are absent
