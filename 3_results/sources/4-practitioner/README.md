# Layer 4: Practitioner essays and clinical writing

Experienced therapists reflecting on long-term work — clinical wisdom outside journal articles.

## Inclusion criteria

- Substantive reflection, not listicles
- Practitioner working with complex developmental trauma (not single-incident)
- Addresses duration, trajectory, or the experience of long-term work explicitly

## Sources to check

- Substack, Medium, Psychology Today blogs
- Psychotherapy Networker long-form articles
- IARPP clinical papers and colloquia
- Mad in America
