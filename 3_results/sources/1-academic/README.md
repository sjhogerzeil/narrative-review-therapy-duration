# Layer 1: Academic and clinical literature

Peer-reviewed articles, clinical books, and theoretical works addressing duration and trajectory of healing from complex developmental trauma.

## Subdirectories

- `trauma-clinical/` — Trauma-specific clinical literature (Herman, van der Hart, Steele, van der Kolk, Fisher, Ogden)
- `psychoanalytic/` — Psychoanalytic and psychodynamic tradition (Kohut, Winnicott, Bromberg, Stolorow, Fonagy). Role: theoretical background for *why* deep change takes years.
- `humanistic-existential/` — Person-centred, existential, EFT (Rogers, Yalom, van Deurzen, Greenberg, Warner)
- `neuroscience/` — Developmental neuroscience (Schore, Porges, Perry, Lanius)
