# Layer 6: Memoirs and autobiographies

First-person books narrating the full arc of healing. These offer the most complete temporal picture — years of work seen from a vantage point of sufficient distance.

## Known candidates

- Stephanie Foo, *What My Bones Know* (2022) — CPTSD memoir, explicit about duration and method-switching
- Peter Levine, *An Autobiography of Trauma* (2024) — developer of SE narrating his own healing
- Pete Walker, *Complex PTSD: From Surviving to Thriving* — part self-help, part autobiographical
- Dina Goldstein (if available — therapist memoir)
