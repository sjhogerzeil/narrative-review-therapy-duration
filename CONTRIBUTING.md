# Contributing

This is a completed structured narrative review. Contributions that strengthen, challenge, or extend the evidence are welcome.

## Feedback and discussion

**GitHub Issues** — for errors, missing sources, disagreements, or questions about the methodology or argument. [Open an issue](https://github.com/sjhogerzeil/narrative-review-therapy-duration/issues) ([how to open an issue](https://docs.github.com/en/issues/tracking-your-work-with-issues/using-issues/creating-an-issue)).

**Email** — for general correspondence: s.j.hogerzeil@gmail.com

## Substantive contributions

**Challenge a finding.** If you know of a source that contradicts or complicates a claim, open an issue with the citation and a brief note on what it changes. Better yet: annotate it using the [source template](3_results/sources/SOURCE_TEMPLATE.md) and submit a [pull request](https://docs.github.com/en/pull-requests/collaborating-with-pull-requests/proposing-changes-to-your-work-with-pull-requests/about-pull-requests).

**Fill an access gap.** Four key sources remain inaccessible (see [`7_process/todo.md`](7_process/todo.md)): Herman (1992), Van der Hart et al. (2006), Yeates et al. (2023), and Farina et al. (2025). If you can provide full texts, open an issue or email the author.

**Replicate independently.** Read the same 89 sources, follow the same annotation protocol, and check whether you arrive at the same synthesis. Agreements and disagreements are both valuable. See [`2_methods/15_reproducibility.md`](2_methods/15_reproducibility.md).

**Fork for a different question.** The CIS methodology, six-layer architecture, and annotation tooling are not specific to therapy duration. See [`2_methods/15_reproducibility.md`](2_methods/15_reproducibility.md) for instructions.
